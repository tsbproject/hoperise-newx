About us

vision statement

"Empowering Lives, Transforming Communities" At our Christian charity, we envision a world where individuals are freed from poverty, living transformed lives, and fulfilling their God-given potential. Our mission is to follow Jesus where the need is greatest, responding to crises and partnering with local churches and organizations to help people lift themselves out of poverty.

mission statement

"Our mission is to empower individuals and families in need by providing them with the necessary skills and knowledge to learn new vocation or trade. Through our religious charity, we strive to create opportunities for personal growth, economic independence, and self-sufficiency.

By offering comprehensive training programs, mentorship, and support, we aim to equip individuals with the tools they need to succeed in their chosen field. Our commitment to compassion, inclusivity, and community drives us to make a positive impact on the lives of those we serve. Showing the Love of God to individuals helping them to know there is hope for their future.

Helping each person where they are at spiritually, emotionally, and physically. Teaching and training each individual to live honestly, with integrity and learn self value so they can know who they are in Christ and know Christ has a plan and a purpose for their lives. Welcome to HopeRise Charity

HopeRise Charity is more than just an organization; it is a heartfelt mission, a divine calling to spread the Love of Christ to humanity every single day. Our story began many years ago, rooted in the compassionate actions of our founder, Folasade A.O. Sowunmi, who was deeply inspired by her father’s unwavering generosity. From the tender age of 14, Folasade witnessed her father give selflessly to those who had nothing—no food, no clothing, no shelter. His daily acts of kindness, meeting the needs of strangers, became a powerful lesson in compassion and service.

Growing up in a home where cooking for and accommodating strangers was a daily routine, Folasade’s heart was shaped by these experiences. She developed a profound passion to help those in need, driven by the grace of God. Over the years, she has engaged in countless acts of charity, often without records or photos, building lasting relationships with those she has helped. Many of these individuals remain a part of her life, bearing testimony to the impact of her compassion.

The plight of the needy, the impoverished, and the homeless often keeps Folasade awake at night, bringing her to tears. Despite doing all she can within her means, she knows there is always more to be done. This is where HopeRise Charity steps in, calling upon the world to join hands in this noble cause. Together, with other organizations, individuals, groups, government agencies, private and public companies, churches, and anyone willing to share even the smallest contribution, we can transform lives and communities.

Our vision is expansive and inclusive: to train and empower people to stand on their own feet, to build emergency housing for those struck by disaster, to support women in learning trades that can sustain their families, to help men gain employable skills, to ensure children can return to school and receive proper nutrition, and to support healthcare centres in providing essential medications. By working with governments and international organizations, we aim to alleviate poverty and bring hope through basic amenities that enable people to work and earn a living. We strive to establish training centres where people can learn for free and improve their lives.

At HopeRise, we believe that hope can rise in any situation. Our mission is to empower individuals and transform communities, creating a brighter future for all.
Join us in this journey of compassion and transformation. Together, we can make a difference.

HopeRise Charity offers a variety of programs designed to support and empower individuals and communities. Here are some of the key programs:

  1. Emergency Housing: Providing immediate shelter for those who are homeless or have been affected by disasters. This program ensures that individuals and families have a safe place to stay while they work towards stability
  2. Vocational Training: Offering training programs to help individuals gain employable skills. This includes workshops and courses in various trades, aimed at helping people secure stable employment and improve their livelihoods.
  3. Women’s Empowerment: Supporting women in learning trades and skills that can help them sustain their families. This program focuses on providing women with the tools and knowledge they need to become financially independent
  4. Children’s Education: Ensuring that children have access to education and proper nutrition. This program helps children return to school and provides the necessary support to keep them there, including school supplies and meals.
  5. Healthcare Support: Assisting healthcare centers in providing basic medications and healthcare services. This program aims to improve access to essential healthcare for underserved communities
  6. Poverty Alleviation: Working with governments and international organizations to bring basic amenities to communities. This includes efforts to improve infrastructure, provide clean water, and create opportunities for economic development.
  7. Community Training Centers: Establishing centers where people can receive free training in various skills. These centers are designed to help individuals improve their lives through education and skill development.

By offering these programs, HopeRise Charity aims to create lasting change and empower individuals to rise above their circumstances. Each program is a step towards building stronger, more resilient communities.

There are many meaningful ways you can contribute to HopeRise Charity and help make a difference in the lives of those in need:

  1. Donation: Financial contributions are always welcome and can be made as a one-time gift or as a recurring donation. Your support helps fund our various programs and initiatives, ensuring we can continue to provide essential services to those in need.
  2. Volunteer: Your time and skills are invaluable. Whether it’s helping out at our shelters, assisting with vocational training programs, or participating in community outreach events, there are numerous opportunities to get involved.
  3. Partner with us: If you represent an organization, business, or community group, consider partnering with HopeRise. Collaborative efforts can amplify our impact and reach more people in need.
  4. Spread the word: Raise awareness about HopeRise and our mission by sharing our story with your network. Follow us on social media, share our posts, and encourage others to get involved.
  5. In-kind donation: Contribute goods and services that can directly benefit those we serve. This could include clothing, food, hygiene products, or professional services like legal advice or medical care.
  6. Corporate giving: : If you own or work for a company, explore options for corporate giving, such as matching gift programs, sponsorships, or employee volunteer days.
  7. Legacy giving: Consider including HopeRise in your will or estate plans. Legacy gifts ensure that your commitment to helping others continues for generations to come

Every contribution, no matter how small, makes a significant impact. Together, we can bring hope and change to those who need it most. Thank you for considering supporting HopeRise Charity.
